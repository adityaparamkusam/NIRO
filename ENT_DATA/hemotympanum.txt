Hemotympanum: Diagnosis and Treatment
Hemotympanum is the presence of blood behind the eardrum. It can result from head trauma, skull fractures, or ear infections. This condition is significant because it may indicate a serious underlying issue that requires medical attention. While not extremely common, hemotympanum can occur in individuals of all ages, especially after head injuries. If you notice blood or a red tinge behind the eardrum, seek medical help promptly to determine the cause and appropriate treatment.
