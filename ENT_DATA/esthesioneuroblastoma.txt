Esthesioneuroblastoma: Diagnosis and Treatment
Esthesioneuroblastoma is a rare type of cancer that starts in the upper part of the nasal cavity. Although uncommon, it's important because it can affect a person's sense of smell and vision due to its location near critical nerves. It comprises less than 3% of all nasal tumors. While it can occur at any age, it's most commonly diagnosed in people aged 40-60. Understanding its symptoms and getting early diagnosis are crucial for effective treatment.
Causes of Esthesioneuroblastoma
Esthesioneuroblastoma is thought to develop due to a combination of genetic mutations, exposure to certain chemicals or viruses, and possibly inherited conditions. Factors contributing to its development may include:
Genetic mutations
Exposure to certain chemicals
Exposure to certain viruses
Possible inherited conditions
