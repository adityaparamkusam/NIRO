Frequently Asked Questions
What is septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy?
Septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy is a surgical procedure that combines correcting a deviated septum and reshaping the nose with reducing the size of the turbinates to improve nasal breathing and appearance.
How does septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy improve breathing?
Septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy improves breathing by correcting a deviated septum to straighten the nasal passages and reducing the size of the turbinates to increase airflow.
Is septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy safe?
Septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy is generally safe. However, like any surgery, there are potential risks and complications. It's essential to discuss these with your surgeon before making a decision.
What are the benefits of combining septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy?
Combining septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy can improve both the appearance of the nose and breathing by correcting structural issues like a deviated septum and enlarged turbinates.
How long does recovery take after septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy?
Recovery after septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy usually takes around 1 to 2 weeks.
What are the risks of septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy?
Risks of septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy include bleeding, infection, scarring, changes in nasal shape, breathing difficulties, and rare complications like septal perforation or loss of smell. Your surgeon will discuss these risks with you before the procedure.
How successful is septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy in improving nasal function?
Septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy can improve nasal function by correcting structural issues like a deviated septum and enlarged turbinates, but success varies depending on individual factors like overall health and post-operative care.
Does septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy require general anesthesia?
Yes, septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy typically requires general anesthesia to ensure the patient remains unconscious and pain-free during the surgery.
Will septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy affect my sense of smell?
Septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy may temporarily affect your sense of smell due to swelling and nasal packing, but it typically returns to normal as you heal.
Is septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy covered by insurance?
Insurance coverage for septorhinoplasty with turbinectomy may vary depending on your insurance plan and the medical necessity of the procedure. It is best to check with your insurance provider to determine coverage.
